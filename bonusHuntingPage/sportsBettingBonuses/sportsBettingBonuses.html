Understanding Betting Odds

Understanding the basics of sports betting odds is crucial for making money through sports betting promotions. Sports betting odds are a way for sportsbooks to communicate the potential payout for a bet. They indicate how much you stand to win relative to your initial bet.

Positive (+) and Negative (-) Odds

When you see positive odds (e.g., +200), they represent the amount of profit you can make on a $100 bet. Essentially, positive odds show how much you stand to win if you bet $100.

For example:

If you see +200 odds, it means that for every $100 you bet, you stand to win $200 if your bet is successful. In total, you'd receive your $100 stake back, plus $200 in profit.

On the other hand, negative odds (e.g., -200) represent how much you need to bet in order to win $100. Essentially, negative odds show the amount you need to risk to earn $100 in profit.

For example:

If you see -200 odds, it means you need to wager $200 in order to make a $100 profit if the bet wins. In this case, if your bet is successful, you’d receive your $200 stake back, plus $100 in profit.

Why Do The Odds Vary?

Odds fluctuate based on various factors, including the teams or players involved, current betting trends, and even the sportsbook's own strategy to attract or limit action on certain events.

Positive odds are typically offered on the underdog in an event, as they represent a less likely outcome. Negative odds are generally offered on the favorite in a given matchup, as they represent a more likely outcome.

Even if you don’t fully grasp every detail of betting odds, the key takeaway is to be aware of minimum odds requirements for sportsbook promotions. For instance, if a promo specifies a minimum of +100, any odds higher than that will typically qualify. Fortunately, sportsbook apps usually calculate and display potential payouts when you place your bet, so having a deep understanding of the odds isn't essential. As long as you understand the basic principles, you'll be equipped to take advantage of promotions effectively.
